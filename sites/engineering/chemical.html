About Chemical Engineering

Chemical Engineering is a branch of engineering that applies physical and chemical processes to convert raw materials into useful products.

Areas of Study

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Heat Transfer
  • Mass Transfer
  • Chemical Reaction Engineering
  • Process Control

Career Opportunities

Chemical engineers work in various industries such as:

  • Chemical Manufacturing
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Energy
  • Food and Beverage
  • Environmental Engineering

Conclusion

Chemical Engineering is a fascinating field that combines science, engineering, and problem-solving skills to create innovative solutions.
