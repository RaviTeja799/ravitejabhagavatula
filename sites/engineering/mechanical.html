Mechanical Engineering

Welcome to the world of mechanical engineering!

Static Equilibrium

When an object is in static equilibrium, the sum of all forces acting on it is zero and the sum of all torques acting on it is also zero.

Stress and Strain

Stress is the force per unit area applied to a material, while strain is the resulting deformation of the material.

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics is the study of energy and its transformations in mechanical systems.

Fluid Mechanics

Fluid mechanics deals with the behavior of fluids (liquids, gases, and plasmas) at rest and in motion.

Mechanical Vibrations

Mechanical vibrations involve the study of oscillations and vibrations in mechanical systems.
